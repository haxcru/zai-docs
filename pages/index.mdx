# What is ZAI?

![zai loves dai](/zai-heart-dai.png)

## Zero Collateral DAI

ZAI is an [open source](https://github.com/zaifinance/zai-protocol) experimental, DAI-pegged algorithmic stablecoin designed to enable
open market activities and capital efficient supply dynamics. At its core, ZAI
is a fork of [Empty Set Dollar (ESD)](https://www.emptyset.finance/) with
mechanics evolving the fundamental components of Stability, Composability, and
Decentralization.
